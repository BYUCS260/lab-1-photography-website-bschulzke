About

Ben Schulzke

Ben Schulzke, creator of Fantastically Photographic Photography, is a mild-mannered citizen heralding from the Pleasant Grove.

Unlike many of his mild-mannered peers, Schulzke has no supernatural abilities, nor is he a billionaire or an orphaned alien.

He's the photographer, of course. But he doesn't really take many pictures.

In fact, what he does is as much a mystery to himself as to his peers.
